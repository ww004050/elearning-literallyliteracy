About Literally Literacy

LiterallyLiteracy is a platform for education. committed to creating the best inside and out learning environment, making LiterallyLiteracy the best place for to study, educate and construct the future of online learning experiences.
Education is old. In the United States, the modern school structure dates back to the 19th century and was not meant to scale the way it has. By improving the way things work in the classroom and by taking the classroom online, several corporations are working to "disrupt" education.

Mission

LiterallyLiteracy aren't one of those companies. From the ground up, LiterallyLiteracy are rethinking schooling. The web has rethought nearly everything - commerce, social networking, healthcare, and more. LiterallyLiteracy are developing the education required by the world - the first genuinely online inclusive learning.
